▶ WHAT IS THE RESELLER TOOLKIT?

The Reseller Toolkit is a free, no-signup web app that calculates your real net profit after eBay, Mercari, and Poshmark fees — and tracks your inventory, item aging, dead stock, and realized profit in one place. Everything runs locally in your browser, so your data stays private.

Use the Calculator tab to see net profit, margin, ROI, and the break-even sale price for any item after marketplace and payment fees. Then hit Add to inventory to track it in the Inventory tab, where items age into fresh, watch, and 90+ day dead-stock buckets and "mark sold" records your true realized profit using the saved fees.

▶ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is the Reseller Toolkit free?

Yes — completely free, with no signup, no account, and no ads. It runs entirely in your browser.

Which marketplaces does it support?

Fee presets for eBay, Mercari, and Poshmark, plus a Custom option with editable fee fields so you can match any platform or your exact category rate.

How does it calculate net profit?

Net profit = sale price − platform fee − fixed per-order fee − shipping − cost of goods − extra costs. It also shows profit margin and ROI.

What is eBay's selling fee in 2026?

eBay's standard final value fee is about 13.25% plus a $0.30 per-order fee, but it varies by category and store subscription. The fee fields are editable so you can enter your exact rate.

Does it track inventory?

Yes — aging buckets (fresh, watch, and 90+ day dead stock), capital tied up, potential revenue, realized profit, and your sell-through rate.

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything is stored only in your browser's local storage. Nothing is uploaded, and there is no tracking or account.

Can I export my data?

Yes — CSV export and import, so you can back up your inventory or move it between devices.
